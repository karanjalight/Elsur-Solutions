Key Modules

Fleet Management

Our system has different features and functionalities like:

  • GPS real-time positioning, route monitoring, and playback.
  • Geo-fencing - both entry and departure zones.
  • Speed monitoring, over-speed reports, and alerts.
  • Immobilize vehicle remotely, engine stop and resume by SMS and GPRS.
  • SMS tracking with Google Maps link.
  • Web server, computer tracking software, and Mobile app.
  • On-board diagnosis (OBD) 2 interface to read data from the vehicle computer.
  • SOS alert/Panic button.
  • Backup battery to track even if vehicle battery is removed.
  • I-button key driver identification.
  • Vehicle activity reports - Engine status reports.
  • Driver Management including driver vehicle allocation and work ticketing.
  • Vehicle expense management.
  • Maintenance/Service reminders.
  • Mileage reports.
  • SMS/Email alerts e.g. overspeeding, geofence violation, fuel pilferage, harsh acceleration/braking, SOS, extended engine idling, towing, crash detection, device disconnection, etc.
