Meet Your Trusted Solar Company in Kenya

At Elsur Renewable Energy (RE) Solutions, we believe in transforming how Kenya powers its future. As a trusted leader in solar power solutions, we provide reliable, affordable, and sustainable energy systems designed to cut electricity costs while protecting the environment.
With over 20MW of active installations across Kenya, Elsur RE Solutions has built a strong reputation for delivering tailor-made solar systems for homes, businesses, industries, and institutions. Our team of qualified engineers, electricians, and solar technicians is dedicated to ensuring every installation is optimized for maximum efficiency and long-term performance.
We are proudly licensed by EPRA (Energy and Petroleum Regulatory Authority) and the exclusive distributor of SunPower solar panels, the world’s most efficient solar technology. Our services go beyond installation—we provide after-sales support, financing options, and maintenance packages to guarantee uninterrupted power and peace of mind.
